About AP2PFS

AP2PFS is a revolutionary lightweight, zero-knowledge file sharing utility that puts privacy first. By leveraging cutting-edge Peer-to-Peer technology, your files are transmitted directly between users without ever touching a central server.

In an era of increasing surveillance and data collection, AP2PFS offers a sanctuary for those who value their digital freedom. Whether you're sharing documents with colleagues, sending photos to friends, or transferring large files across the globe, AP2PFS ensures your data remains yours alone.

  • Complete Anonymity: No logs, no accounts, no tracking. Your identity and activities remain completely private.
  • True P2P: Direct transfer between users means no middleman can intercept, monitor, or store your files.
  • Zero Knowledge: Even we don't know what you're sharing. Your data is encrypted end-to-end.
